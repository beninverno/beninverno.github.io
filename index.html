Once programmed, i will lay the tube around the bottom edges of the room and plug it in, and hopefully it will be able to light it up with some cool colors!

I’ve always wanted to work with a micro-computer (such as Arduino or Raspberry Pi) to create something. I’ve had some experience with coding so I believe this will be the next step in getting better with coding.
